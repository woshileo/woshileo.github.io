Introduction

Welcome to my blog about my trip to Turkey! I was there from May 22nd, 2026 to May 30th, 2026 — 5 nights in Istanbul followed by 3 nights in Cappadocia (Göreme).

Istanbul

Home base was Galata / Şişhane in Beyoğlu, right next to the Galata Tower.

Date Itinerary
Friday, May 22 (arrival)
  • Land at Istanbul Airport at 6:36pm
  • Check in near the Galata Tower (Büyük Hendek Cd)
  • First evening out in Galata
Saturday, May 23 (Bosphorus & the Asian side)
  • Karaköy waterfront on the Golden Horn
  • Ferry across to Kadıköy on the Asian side
  • Beylerbeyi Palace + a stretch of the Bosphorus by boat
  • Kuzguncuk — the pastel waterfront neighborhood
  • Yıldız Park in Beşiktaş
  • Evening back in Tünel / Galata, late night out on İlk Belediye Cd
Sunday, May 24 (Historic Peninsula)
  • Sultanahmet: the Blue Mosque, Hagia Sophia, and Sultanahmet Park
  • Lunch in the restaurant lanes just south of the Blue Mosque
  • Long afternoon in Gülhane Park
  • Evening show at Garibaldi Salon in Asmalımescit
Monday, May 25 (Grand Bazaar & the Golden Horn)
  • Nuruosmaniye Mosque + Bayezid Mosque
  • Grand Bazaar (Kapalıçarşı)
  • Walk past Hagia Sophia, then across the Galata Bridge to Karaköy for lunch
  • Fener / Balat — the colorful old quarter on the Golden Horn
  • Meyhane (taverna) dinner & rakı on Sofyalı Sk in Asmalımescit
  • Nightcap back in Galata
Tuesday, May 26 (Galata Tower & up the Bosphorus)
  • Up the Galata Tower in the morning
  • Café hopping in Cihangir all afternoon
  • Evening up the European shore: Beşiktaş → Arnavutköy → Bebek for a Bosphorus fish dinner

Cappadocia

Home base was Göreme village in Nevşehir, in the heart of the fairy-chimney valleys.

Date Itinerary
Wednesday, May 27 (fly to Cappadocia)
  • Morning flight, land at Cappadocia Airport (Nevşehir) around 1pm
  • Göreme Open-Air Museum + the national park valleys
  • Settle in and dinner in Göreme village
Thursday, May 28 (the big Cappadocia day)
  • Sunrise in the valleys at dawn — hot-air balloon hour
  • Breakfast back in Göreme
  • Uçhisar Castle
  • Derinkuyu Underground City
  • Sunset at Göreme Sunset Point
  • Dinner in Göreme village
Friday, May 29 (Ihlara Valley day trip)
  • Drive west through Uçhisar toward Aksaray
  • Selime Monastery — the huge rock-cut monastery
  • Ihlara Valley — the green river canyon walk
  • Final evening in Göreme
Saturday, May 30 (heading home)
  • One more sunrise in the Göreme valleys
  • Fly Cappadocia → Istanbul → home

Highlights

  • Istanbul neighborhoods: Beyoğlu, Karaköy, Kadıköy, Üsküdar, Kuzguncuk, Beşiktaş, Bebek, Sultanahmet, Fatih, Fener-Balat
  • Cappadocia: Göreme, Uçhisar, Derinkuyu, Ihlara Valley & Selime Monastery
  • Favorite landmarks: Blue Mosque, Hagia Sophia, Grand Bazaar, Galata Tower, Beylerbeyi Palace, Gülhane Park · Göreme Open-Air Museum, Uçhisar Castle, Derinkuyu Underground City, Selime Monastery, Ihlara Valley
  • Most memorable: sunrise hot-air balloons over the Göreme valleys
